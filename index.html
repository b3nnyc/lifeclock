What is Life Clock?

Life Clock is your first step in determining how you, and your family members, can consciously, powerfully, and effectively learn to Live the Legacy You Want to Leave. It helps you find your direction when you are looking for a powerful way of moving forward.

The 12 most important words in life are: I love you; I forgive you; Thank you; We're okay; I'm okay….Are these 12 words ones which you can easily say and mean?

Life Clock works with individuals and families to help deal with issues relating to:

  • building and strengthening trusting relationships
  • conflict resolution
  • personal empowerment
  • living life with fewer regrets
  • increasing resilience
  • finding your personal path forward in any area of your life
  • honing financial responsibility
  • strategic thinking
  • continuity planning and implementation
  • philanthropy
  • good governance for family businesses
  • coping with illness
  • dealing with grief
  • having a good death

Who Life Clock serves

Life Clock works with the young, those getting older, the people transitioning their roles, the retired, the retiring, families, individuals, with people who are grieving, and with the ill. Work is done in service of:

  • facilitating important conversation
  • improving relationships
  • effectively navigating family tensions
  • creating opportunities to alleviate regrets and guilt
  • aiding with personal indiscretion management in a confidential way that maintains dignity and integrity
  • ensuring you keep your rights, agency, and ability to make good decisions.

Taking time to set your house in order becomes not just something that you'd like to do one day, but holds greater urgency as you get older, wish to retire, and/or become ill. It is a gift you give yourself, your family, your company, your friends, and your community.
