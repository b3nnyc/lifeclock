Retiring, aging, illness - putting your affairs in order

The kindest gift you can give to others is putting your affairs in order. For this to occur, you must share your choices.

Do you…

  • Do you know your wishes for your death, your estate, and your legacy?
  • Do you know if others were asked, what would they say?
  • Do others know your lawyer, your electronic passwords, where critical documents are kept, your bank, the bills that require payment each month…?
  • Do you have unsaid but important conversations you wish you could have?

Did you know…

  • More than 125 million N. American citizens have no will.
  • 1/10 people die without any warning (stroke, heart attack, homicide, suicide).
  • More than 1/2 of all people die without discussing critical issues (e.g.: wishes for their family, business, estate, or end of life.)
  • Grief is longer when things have been left unsaid, or conflict hasn’t had a chance to heal.
